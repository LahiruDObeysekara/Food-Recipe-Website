Pol Sambol

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh coconut, grated
  • 1 medium-sized red onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1-2 red chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 lime or lemon, juiced
  • Salt, to taste

    • Procedure:

    1. Prepare Coconut: Grate the fresh coconut and set it aside.
    2. Mix Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated coconut, finely chopped red onion, green chilies, red chilies, tomato, and curry leaves.
    3. Seasoning: Add salt to taste. Start with a small amount and adjust according to your preference.
    4. Mix Well: Gently mix all the ingredients until well combined. The heat from the chilies and the acidity from the lime will enhance the flavors over time.
    5. Add Lime Juice: Squeeze the juice of half a lime or lemon into the mixture. Adjust the quantity based on your preference for acidity.
    6. Taste and Adjust: Taste the Pol Sambol and adjust the salt or lime juice if needed. You can also add more chilies for extra heat.
    7. Rest: Allow the Pol Sambol to rest for at least 15-30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld together.
    8. Serve: Pol Sambol is ready to be served! It's a fantastic accompaniment to rice and curry dishes or as a spicy condiment for other meals.
