Fish AmbulThiyal

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup goraka, dried and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fenugreek seeds
  • 10-15 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 medium-sized onions, thinly sliced
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Goraka Paste: Soak the sliced goraka in a little water to soften. Grind it into a smooth paste. Set aside.
  2. Fry Fenugreek Seeds: In a dry pan, lightly roast the fenugreek seeds until they release a nutty aroma. Be careful not to burn them. Grind the roasted fenugreek seeds into a fine powder. Set aside.
  3. Sauté Onions, Garlic, and Ginger: In a large pot or pan, heat vegetable oil. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add minced garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add Goraka Paste and Spices: Add the goraka paste to the sautéed onions, along with ground turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the mixture releases its aroma.
  5. Add Fish and Curry Leaves: Add fish cubes to the mixture and coat them evenly with the spice mixture. Add curry leaves for flavor.
  6. Cook Until Fish Changes Color: Cook the fish over medium heat, stirring gently to avoid breaking the fish cubes. Continue cooking until the fish changes color and gets coated with the spice mixture.
  7. Add Water and Simmer: Pour enough water to cover the fish. Bring it to a boil and then reduce the heat. Allow it to simmer until the fish is cooked and the curry thickens to your desired consistency.
  8. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Add more salt or spice if required.
  9. Serve: Fish Ambul Thiyal is ready to be served! It pairs well with steamed rice and other Sri Lankan dishes.
